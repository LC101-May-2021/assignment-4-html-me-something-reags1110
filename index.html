Reagan's Dream Passport...

These are some of my dream destinations that I haven't been to...YET!

My Travel List:

  • Venice
  • Paris
  • Hawaii
  • Alaska

Venice

If I had to choose just one destination on this list, it would be Venice . I fell in love with this transcendent city long ago and, as I grew older, have dreamt of sitting in a gondola floating on the Grand Canal just letting the world go by. There’s something extremely romantic about Venice that makes you want to experience it with the one you love. I so want my husband and I to go there, emmerse ourselves in this magnificent city, and then relax with a Bellini (created at the world famous Harry's Bar!) and just enjoy life like only Venetians can. Just imagine strolling down alleyways, eating authentic gelato while window shopping for Italian leather handbags (or shoes!!). Whether it’s the historic Basilica located in the heart of the Piazza San Marco, the Rialto Bridge, or the Ponte dei Sospiri, there is so much history to be seen on this magical little island, and I want to experience it all.

Paris

Paris, France...honestly, are those the most romantic words you've ever heard? I've always wanted to see what draws people to this magnificent "City of Lights" and experience, first hand, the vastness of culture and history that only Paris offers. Although most travel here for the sites and sounds (and rightly so), my reasons would be a little different with this one; it's the food!! I'm serious. The French, hands down in my opinion, are the crème de la crème(see what I did there?) when it comes to cuisine. I want to sit in a Parisian cafe, sipping on a cafè au lait and nibbling on a croissant. To walk hand in hand with my husband to a pâtisserie and decide on which eclair to devour, or to buy a baguette from a vendor, stroll along the Left Bank and just...breath. And, let's be honest, if the Eiffel Tower is in the background with all of this, that would be absolutely majestic all on it's own.

Hawaii

From the incredible beaches, to the majestic waterfalls, Hawaii has a little bit of everything that you need for an epic tropical vacation. This is a place that comes to mind when somebody asks me "Where would you go to lay on a beach and chill?". I mean does it get any better than Hawaii? I want to visit Maui the most because of the waterfalls. WOW are they stunning! My ideal Hawaiian getaway would have to include just lying on the beach, listening to the waves, sipping on a Mai Tai and doing absolutely nothing. Heavenly, right? My thoughts exactly. Aloha!

Alaska

I've heard you feel small in Alaska because of how open it is. Just to experience that, in itself, would be incredible. To be able to witness the astounding scenery that Alaska offers us, whether it be the mountains or fishing, would just be a dream. I know it'll be cold, and I'm working through that, but I think it'll be worth it when I'm looking up at the Northern Lights after whale watching all day and just being in awe of everything. Also added bonus: Crab and Salmon. To be able to have those freshly caught with that scenery around me while I indulge on life? That's a no brainer.
